We are Fremmed, a post-punk metal band hailing from Denmark and the Faroe Islands, that are currently based in Copenhagen. Our drive is to make sense of the world, which each of us struggles with from time to time. For us, to make music is one way of 'interpreting' an undefinable sense of not belonging anywhere.

The emotional experience of homelessness and loneliness is not always something that can be put into words - and in that way, their music can express it without interpreting it.

History

The band was formed in 2013 by the lead singer, Atli Kamban, guitarist, Niels Asger Svensson and and Linus Nicolaj Carlsen who plays the drums.

Fremmed has been in a process of forming since a few of the members met in high school, having gone through all sort of shifts and changes to reach the point where they are today. They have been experimenting with different forms of music for years, starting from a folkish sound to the post punk metal you hear today.

They have had several name changes in the past as they started out with Drowned, then Drowned Sessions, but with the shift in their music they felt it was time to reflect those changes in the band's name and identity - welcoming Fremmed.

Releases

Fremmed released an EP called Conversation with Cora in 2013. Later, in 2014 they released Horses, and then their full length album, called Home to the Wind.
